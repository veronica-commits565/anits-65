About the training and placements

Founded by NRI philanthropist Dr. N.B.R. Prasad in loving memory of his late son, Anil Neerukonda, the institution is situated on a sprawling 12-acre campus. ANITS is permanently affiliated with Andhra University (AU) and has steadily grown into a hub of academic innovation, modern infrastructure, and research-driven learning. Through robust support from the Training and Placement Department, students frequently secure top-tier placements, with the highest package reaching Rs 12 lakhs. The college also operates as a recognized Business Incubator under the MSME Incubation Scheme, heavily fostering entrepreneurship and technical excellence.
